Don't reinvent the wheel

At some point this will probably turn into a blog, but on a daily basis, there is always piles of stuff to fix. At the moment, while this site is a refugee on a Raspberry Pi, it's natural host, a Bubba server is being upgraded from Debian Etch to at least Squeeze if not Wheezy.

Etch to Squeeze or Wheezy is a long journey, made no easier by software selections that regularly required the forcing of selections. See this page for details.

The idea here is to dig out all those archives and pendrives with their hundreds and hundreds of hacks, fixes, discoveries and other gems, show them to the world, and save myself some headaches.
